My top 3 movies of all time

The Danish Girl

Eddie Redmayne nailed the role. It was very emotional and touching.

Shutter Island

Any movie directed by Scorsese is sure to be a treat, and DiCaprio's acting was impeccable as usual.The storyline in itself was just unpredictable and had a major twist at the end, left me feeling disoriented for a few days after.

Forest Gump

This was a vey touching movie and is close to my heart as i had watched it during a time when i wasn't mentally stable and it provided me comfort.
